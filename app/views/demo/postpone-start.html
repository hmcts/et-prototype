Apply to postpone my hearing

Use this form to ask the tribunal to postpone your hearing to a later date.

Details to include in your application:

  • which hearing you want to postpone. If the hearing you want to postpone is listed over multiple days, tell us which day or days you are applying to postpone
  • the reason you want to postpone. The tribunal will use this to decide whether to grant your application
  • any essential documents to support your application. For example, you may want to upload a medical note or correspondence from a witness who cannot attend
  • weekday dates within the next 3 months that you, your witnesses or representatives cannot attend a rescheduled hearing
  • the reason you cannot attend on those dates

Upload letters, photos or documents if they are relevant to your application. Do not upload any other material.

Remember that:

  • if you are taking a picture of a letter, place it on a flat surface and take the picture from above
  • if you are uploading written documents with tracked changes, make sure that tracked changes are turned on
  • you can make more than one application at the same time by completing each form separately. If the applications are linked you can make that clear in the text box
Give details of your application. Please state which respondent or respondents your application applies to. Use both the box and the file upload if you need to.
