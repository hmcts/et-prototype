Apply to restrict publicity in my case

Use this form to apply to restrict publicity in your case.

It is an important principle that justice should normally be delivered in public.

However, the tribunal has the power under Rule 50 to prevent or restrict the public disclosure of any aspect of your case if necessary in the interests of justice, or to protect the Convention rights of any person.

What are Convention rights?
Convention rights are the rights and fundamental freedoms as set out in the Human Rights Act 1998.
You will be able to review your application before you submit it.
