Hearing format

If this case goes to a hearing, it can take place either:

  • in person, in a room at a tribunal (‘face-to-face’)
  • by video (where you can join from a place suitable to you)
  • by phone

Some hearings use a combination of these methods. The approach taken will be decided by a judge. It may depend on the type of claim.
