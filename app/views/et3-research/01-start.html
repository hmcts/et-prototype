Response to ET1 employment tribunal claim (ET3)

This response must be completed and submitted within 28 days of the date of the claim form being sent by the tribunal.

You must provide your response to the claim even if you believe that another respondent is liable.

If you do not provide a response, a judgment may be issued against you without a hearing.

If you consider another person or company may be liable, you should still submit a response. You will have a chance in this response to explain why you think someone else may be liable.

You will need:

  • the claimant's employment start and end dates, hours of work and notice period
  • the claimant's pay and benefits, before and after tax
  • your detailed response to the claim

Remember that:

  • you do not have to answer questions that are labelled optional
  • you can review and edit your answers before you submit the form
