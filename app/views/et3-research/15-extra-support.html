Extra support during the case

We know some people need support to access information and use our services. We call this a reasonable adjustment. Some reasonable adjustments need to be agreed by a judge or HMCTS. You can discuss with the tribunal if your needs change.
What support is available?

Reasonable adjustments can include:

  • documents in alternative formats, colours and fonts
  • help with communicating, sight, hearing, speaking and interpretation
  • access and mobility support if a hearing takes place in person

Contact
